What We Do

Our Trading Operations

Aurore Commodities is a principal trading company in the international petroleum products market. We source, structure and deliver crude oil and refined products directly to buyers across multiple continents and jurisdictions.

Our operations span the full lifecycle of each transaction, from initial commercial engagement, counterparty due diligence and contract structuring through to logistics coordination, inspection, documentation and final settlement.

We operate on both a spot and term basis, with the flexibility to structure transactions around our clients' specific volume requirements, delivery schedules and commercial preferences. All deliveries are supported by internationally recognised inspection agencies and accompanied by full shipping and quality documentation.

Products We Trade

Aurore Commodities supplies a comprehensive range of petroleum products to commercial and industrial buyers worldwide. Each product is available in volumes and specifications tailored to the requirements of the transaction.

Crude Oil

Light and heavy crude grades sourced from established production regions, with flexible volume commitments and delivery terms structured to meet each client's operational requirements.

Diesel

High-quality diesel fuel supplied to international standards including EN590, D2 and other regional specifications, available for bulk delivery to commercial and industrial buyers.

Gasoline

Motor gasoline in a range of octane grades and specifications, supplied to wholesalers, distributors and industrial end-users across international markets.

Aviation Fuel

Jet A-1 and other aviation turbine fuel grades for airlines, airport fuel supply operations and into-plane services, delivered to specification with full quality documentation.

Fuel Oil

Heavy fuel oil and low-sulphur fuel oil for marine bunkering, power generation and industrial applications. Available in HSFO and VLSFO grades to meet current IMO regulations.

Naphtha

Light and heavy naphtha grades for petrochemical feedstock and gasoline blending applications, sourced from refineries and delivered to specification.

LPG

Liquefied petroleum gas including propane and butane for domestic, commercial and industrial applications, supplied in bulk via vessel or terminal.

Bitumen

Penetration-grade and polymer-modified bitumen for road construction, waterproofing and industrial applications, available in bulk and drummed packaging.

Our Trading Process

A structured, transparent process from initial engagement through to final settlement.

01

Engagement

Initial consultation to understand your requirements, followed by full counterparty due diligence and compliance verification.

02

Commercial Offer

We issue a detailed commercial offer outlining product specifications, pricing, volume and delivery terms for your review.

03

Contract Execution

Sale and purchase agreement executed, with financial instruments and payment mechanisms arranged through nominated banking channels.

04

Delivery

Full logistics coordination including vessel chartering, transfer operations and terminal management to ensure on-time delivery.

05

Settlement

All shipping and commercial documentation presented, payment settled and transaction formally closed.
