About Aurore Commodities

Our Story

"Aurore" is the French word for dawn, the first light that breaks through darkness. It reflects our commitment to transparency, to clarity of purpose and to the energy that powers global commerce.

Aurore Commodities was founded with a singular purpose: to supply international buyers with crude oil and refined petroleum products through well-structured, commercially sound transactions. Our leadership team brings extensive experience spanning finance, investment management, capital markets and the global energy sector.

Headquartered in the Cayman Islands, we serve clients across multiple continents and jurisdictions. Every transaction we undertake is governed by international trade law, supported by established banking relationships and executed with the highest standards of contractual rigour, compliance and counterparty diligence.

We are not a brokerage. Aurore Commodities is a principal trading company that takes title to the products it sells, bearing the associated commercial risk and delivering the accountability that our clients expect.

Our Mission

To be the trusted supplier of choice for petroleum products in international markets, delivering consistent quality, competitive pricing and operational excellence with integrity, efficiency and transparency at the core of every transaction we execute.

Our Approach

The international oil market is defined by complexity. Cross-border regulations, fluctuating prices, intricate logistics chains and the critical importance of counterparty trust all demand a level of professionalism and discipline that many market participants struggle to deliver consistently.

Aurore Commodities was built to operate at this level. We apply the same institutional rigour to a petroleum transaction that our leadership team previously applied to managing capital, structuring investments and navigating regulated financial markets.

This means thorough due diligence on every counterparty before a contract is signed. It means clear, well-drafted agreements that anticipate and address the risks inherent in physical commodity trading. It means established relationships with inspection agencies, shipping operators and banking institutions that allow us to execute complex, multi-leg transactions with confidence.

The result is a trading operation that our clients can rely on, transaction after transaction.
