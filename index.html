A Trusted Name in International Oil Trading

Aurore Commodities is an international oil and commodities trading company headquartered in the Cayman Islands. We specialise in the sourcing, sale and physical delivery of crude oil and a comprehensive range of refined petroleum products to commercial and industrial buyers worldwide.

Our clients include national oil companies, large-scale distributors, refinery operators and industrial consumers across Europe, Africa, the Middle East, Asia-Pacific and the Americas. Every transaction we undertake is structured with precision, governed by international trade law and supported by established banking and financial instruments.

We exist to provide our clients with a dependable, commercially competitive source of petroleum products, delivered on time, to specification and within the terms agreed.

What We Trade

Aurore Commodities operates across the full spectrum of petroleum products. We supply everything from crude oil to specialist refined fuels, meeting international quality standards and commercial requirements at every level of the supply chain.

Crude Oil

We supply a broad range of crude oil grades to refiners and industrial buyers worldwide, with flexible volume commitments and delivery terms tailored to each transaction.

Refined Products

From diesel and gasoline to aviation fuel, fuel oil and petrochemical feedstocks, we deliver refined products that meet international specifications.

Logistics & Delivery

We manage the full supply chain from origin to destination, coordinating vessel chartering, ship-to-ship transfers and terminal operations worldwide.

Why Aurore Commodities

In a market defined by complexity, counterparty risk and logistical challenges, our clients choose Aurore for the consistency, diligence and commercial discipline we bring to every transaction.

01

Contractual Rigour

Every sale and purchase agreement is structured with meticulous attention to terms, conditions and risk allocation. We work with established legal frameworks and internationally recognised trade instruments to protect the interests of all parties.

02

Financial Strength

Our transactions are underpinned by credible financial instruments including letters of credit, standby letters of credit and bank guarantees arranged through reputable international banking institutions.

03

Operational Capability

From vessel chartering and STS transfer coordination to terminal management and customs documentation, we have the operational infrastructure to execute complex, multi-jurisdictional deliveries.

04

Compliance & Governance

We maintain rigorous KYC and AML procedures, full sanctions screening and transparent corporate governance. Regulatory compliance is embedded in every stage of our operations.
